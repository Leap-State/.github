# Leap-State

Leap-State is an experimental app factory focused on building small applications, tools, and prototypes through exploratory and non-linear thinking.

Some projects start as ideas.
Some start as problems.
Some never become products — by design.

The goal is not scale or speed, but understanding what is worth building.

## What Lives Here

- Small applications  
- Experimental tools  
- Prototypes and proof-of-concepts  
- Early-stage ideas that may evolve into apps  

Projects may be incomplete, paused, or abandoned as part of the exploration process.

## Approach

Leap-State operates with:
- no fixed roadmap  
- no commercial pressure  
- no obligation to ship  

Applications emerge from curiosity, experimentation, and iteration.

## Status

Early stage.
A few applications exist.
More will be added as ideas mature.
